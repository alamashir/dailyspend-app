## [1.0.0] - TBD

### Added
- Voice input for hands-free expense tracking with speech recognition
- AI chat assistant for natural language expense entry (powered by Google Gemini)
- Interactive analytics dashboard with category breakdowns and spending trends
- Monthly budget tracking with visual progress indicators and alerts
- 8 pre-built expense categories: Food & Dining, Transportation, Shopping, Entertainment, Bills & Utilities, Healthcare, Groceries, Other
- Custom category creation and management
- Store name tracking and autocomplete
- Date selection for expenses (with "today" and "yesterday" quick options)
- CSV export for spreadsheet analysis
- JSON export for complete data backups
- Native share functionality (email, AirDrop, etc.)
- Light and dark theme support with system sync
- Currency selection (25+ currencies supported)
- Date format customization
- Native haptic feedback
- Offline-first architecture with IndexedDB storage

### Privacy & Security
- 100% local data storage - no cloud sync
- No user accounts or authentication required
- No usage tracking, analytics, or telemetry
- No ads or subscriptions
- Transparent AI usage disclosure (only chat messages sent for processing)
- All financial data encrypted and stays on device

### Platform Support
- iOS 17.0+ (iPhone)
- Android support coming in future release
